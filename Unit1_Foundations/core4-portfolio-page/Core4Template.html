Reflection

Accessibility

I have adapted my website in many ways striving to make it user-friendly and accessible to all. One of the ways I have attempted to do this is by declaring the language of my website. This will allow for people that use screen readers to have a better experience. I have also added the "alt" attribute which gives a small description of the picture displayed so that screen readers can read them outloud. This attribute can also benefit people that have a slow internet connection.
